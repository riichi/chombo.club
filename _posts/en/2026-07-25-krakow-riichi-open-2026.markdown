## USMA Cup

This tournament is part of the **USMA Cup** — a series of 4 tournaments, from which your best 3 results count toward the overall ranking. Top 3 players will receive prizes.

* MERS: **NON-MERS** Tournament (does not qualify for European ranking)
* USMA Ranking: **Yes**
* USMA Cup: **Yes**

the nearest stop is **Zabłocie**, example connections from the Main Railway Station:

* **Tram 50** from **Dworzec Główny Tunel** stop, approximately 10 minutes
* **Tram 20** from **Teatr Słowackiego** stop, also approximately 10 minutes

The building is right next to the stop — just cross the zebra crossing.
